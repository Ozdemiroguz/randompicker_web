Support & FAQ

Find answers to common questions

How do I create a custom list?

Go to the Custom Lists section, tap the "+" button, enter a name for your list, add items, and save. You can use emojis and icons to make your lists more visual.

Can I use my custom lists in multiple tools?

Yes! Once you create a custom list, you can use it in Wheel Spinner, Select Multiple, Shuffle, Group Maker, and Pairing tools.

Is my data stored online?

No, all your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any information online.

How many items can I add to a list?

There's no limit! You can add as many items as you want to any list, and create unlimited lists and categories.

Can I share my lists with others?

Currently, lists are stored locally on your device. We're working on sharing features for future updates.

How do I use the Wheel Spinner?

Simply add your options to the wheel, then tap the spin button. The wheel will spin and randomly select one of your options. You can customize colors and add emojis to make it more fun!

What's the difference between Shuffle and Select Multiple?

Shuffle randomizes the order of all items in your list. Select Multiple randomly chooses a specified number of items from your list without changing the order of the remaining items.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! The app works completely offline. All features are available without an internet connection since everything is stored locally on your device.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you have the latest version installed. You can also contact us through the contact form below and we'll help you resolve it.

How do I delete my custom lists?

Go to the Custom Lists section, find the list you want to delete, and use the delete option. You can also clear all data through the app settings.
