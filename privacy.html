Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Random Picker – Tap & Decide ("the App", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App.

1. Data Collection

1.1. No Personal Data Collected (Default Behavior)

The App is designed to work fully offline.

By default, the App does not:

  • collect personal information
  • store any user data
  • track users
  • send data to any server
  • require login or account creation

Your lists, preferences, and history remain on your device only.

1.2. Device Data

The App does not access:

  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Location
  • Camera
  • Microphone

unless a future feature explicitly requires it and you give permission.

Currently, these permissions are not used.

2. Local Storage

The App may store items such as:

  • your custom lists
  • recent results
  • app settings

All of this data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1. Advertising (Future Versions)

A future update may include:

  • Google AdMob
  • Apple Search Ads

If enabled, these services may collect:

  • device identifiers
  • approximate usage information

You will be notified of these changes, and the policy will be updated.

Currently, no ads are used.

3.2. Analytics (Future Versions)

In a future update, we may use:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • App Store analytics

These services help us improve the App but do not collect personally identifiable information unless you explicitly consent.

Currently, no analytics tools are active.

4. Purchases & Subscriptions (Future Versions)

If the App introduces a PRO upgrade:

  • all purchases are handled securely by Apple/Google
  • we do not collect or process your payment information
  • subscription status is managed by App Store / Play Store systems

Currently, no purchases exist.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as new features are added.

You will be notified of major changes, and the updated policy will be available inside the App and on our website.
